    Kingdom 123 Rules

    0. Form a committee of two people from each alliance with a castle.

    a. Discuss rules on territory, rules on hives, rules on siege, and sanctions against them.

    b. Discuss the use of the Kingdom Buff and the Kingdom Festival.

    c. This rule may be amended by the Committee's discussions.

    1. The Resource-Gathering Rules

    a.The territory of each alliance is every castle that they occupy, and the land (when clicked) that states the castle’s name

    b. Alliances are guaranteed the resource tiles in their occupied areas.

    c. Do not collect resources from other alliance’s territory

    d.You are free to attack anyone who attempts to gather resources in the castle region occupied by your alliance

    e. The person who collects resources from neutral lands first has priority and cannot be attacked while collecting.

    f. Revenge is also prohibited when neutral resources are attacked.

    g. Under no circumstances is anyone to attack any resource-gathering units outside of the above listed rule – even during wars

    Exception 1: Public enemies

    Exception 2: Declaration from King

    2. Hive Rule

    a. Hives are recognized only when at least 10 castles are together, The attack of a hive is strictly forbidden

    b. Hives cannot be created within the territory occupied by another alliance (including the eight castles).

    Exception1: Free Attack Day

    Exception2: Lord of lords event: Six-stage Lord of war period (all NAPs and Unions are suspended for the duration of this event only)

    3. Siege Rules

    a.During the siege, a form of Hive that is made to attack castles of other alliances is recognized..

    b. If the occupied territory is won by another alliance during the siege, the losing alliance must leave within 24 hours, and the newly occupied alliance cannot attack until after this time period.

    c. After 24 hours, any remaining members from the previous occupying alliance can be attacked

    4.In Case of Rule Violation

    a. In the case of an individual's violation of the rules, the first warning is by mail. the second warning is kicked from the alliance. And he cant join committee alliance with out discuss.

    b. In case of alliance's rule violation, they become the public enemy and target of all kingdom 123 alliances' attacks.

    5. Official PO Buffs Rules

    a.The Protocol Officer (PO) account is opperated by members of the royal committee.

    b.PO account allow access to the titles of construction, research and training of soldiers buffs.

    c.All alliances, except public enemies, should be given titles without discrimination.

    d. An alliance that wants a title buff should invite PO accounts by creating chat rooms by their alliance name+buff.

    6.Free Attack Day (FDA)

    a.FDA will be held on the 1st Sunday (GMT 0:00 to 23:59) of every month.

    7.KVK

    a. One hour before KVK starts, you can attack anyone who doesn't use the truce item.

    b. The reason is to prevent the opponent from scoring after KVK starts.
