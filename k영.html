    Kingdom 123 Rules

    0. Conflict between allies

    a. In the event of a conflict between alliances, they are free to resolve within the scope of the rules below.

    b. The king may intervene if it is considered a serious matter.

    1. The Resource-Gathering Rules

    a. The territory of the alliance is any place where the name of the castle occupied by the alliance is displayed upon clicking on the territory.

    b.The occupying alliance is guaranteed to collect resources from the occupied territory.

    c. The collection of resources within the territory of other alliances is prohibited.

    d.Other allies may attack without prior warning if they attempt to extract or attempt resources from the province of the castle occupied by us.

    e. In the case of neutral resource collection, the one who started first has priority.

    f.Under no circumstances can a resource-collecting unit be attacked when collecting resources in neutral or self-territory.

    g. Retaliation against resource attacks in neutral areas is also prohibited.

    Exception 1: Public enemies

    Exception 2: Declaration from King

    2. Hive Rule

    a.At least 10 allies of the alliance must be gathered in the main castle to be recognized as a hive, and the advance of the hive is absolutely prohibited.

    Exception 1: Hive formed in neutral or other areas are not recognized and can be attacked at all times.

    Therefore, it is recommended to merge with major allies.

    b. Hive cannot be created within the territory occupied by other allies (8 including KL).

    (However, it is possible under the approval of the occupied lord and is not included in the target of attack.)

    Exception1: Free Attack Day

    Exception2: Lord of lords event: Six-stage Lord of war period (all NAPs and Unions are suspended for the duration of this event only)

    3. Siege Rules

    a.. During the siege period, the hive form created to attack other alliance sites is not illegal.

    b. After the siege, if the castle's owner changes, the previous alliance must leave within 24 hours and the newly captured alliance cannot attack.

    Exception1: Even after 24 hours, it is possible to attack a user who does not leave.

    c. During a siege, an alliance that wants a castle can freely occupy it.

    d. Hive attacks during siege are prohibited.

    4.In Case of Rule Violation

    a.In the case of an individual's violation of rules, the first gives a warning and the second leaves the alliance. (Join to other alliances is prohibited.)

    b. Violations of the rules of the alliance become public enemies and are attacked by all allies. (This applies even if the main castle is occupied.)

    5. Official PO Buffs Rules

    a.The Political Officer (PO) account is elected by two from the main alliance to fulfill its mission.

    b.The PO account has access only to the titles of construction, research and soldier training.

    c.The title must be awarded without discrimination to all allies except the public enemy.

    d. Allies who want a title buff should invite a PO account by creating a chat room with the name of the alliance +buff.

    6.Free Attack Day (FDA)

    a. Free attack days are held during the first Sunday of each month. (GMT 0:00 to 23:59)

    7.KVK

    a. 30 minutes before the start of KVK, it is possible to attack truce item non-users. (GMT 0:00 to 0:30)

    b. This is to prevent the opponent server or the opponent player from scoring after the KVK starts.

    c. After the KVK, the main castle's authority occupied by the opponent server is maintained by the alliance captured during the pre-KVK siege.
